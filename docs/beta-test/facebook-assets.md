## üí° FACEBOOK-SPECIFIC ENGAGEMENT TIPS

### Best Practices:

1. **Posting Time**:
   - Best: Weekdays 12-1 PM (lunch break) or 5-7 PM (post-work)
   - Avoid: Early morning (Mon-Fri), late night

2. **Group Posting Strategy**:
   - Read group rules first (many ban self-promotion)
   - Lead with value, not promotion
   - Engage genuinely with other posts before posting
   - Respond to every comment within 1 hour

3. **Album Post Sequence**:
   - Post all 3 images as an album (better reach than single images)
   - Order: OCR transformation ‚Üí AI demo ‚Üí Analytics dashboard
   - This tells a story: Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Results

4. **Comment Engagement**:
   - Reply to every comment (boosts post visibility)
   - Ask follow-up questions
   - Be helpful, not salesy
   - Thank people genuinely
