## 1. What Beta Testing Means

### Your Role

As a beta tester, you are:
- **An early explorer**: You'll use features before anyone else
- **A quality tester**: Your job is to find bugs and report them
- **A product advisor**: Your feedback shapes what we build next
- **A real user**: Use the app as you normally would in your training

### What We're Looking For

‚úÖ **Honest feedback** - Tell us what's broken, confusing, or missing
‚úÖ **Real-world usage** - Use it for actual workouts, not just poking around
‚úÖ **Bug reports** - If something doesn't work, we need to know
‚úÖ **Feature ideas** - What would make this invaluable to you?

‚ùå **Not looking for** - Politeness over honesty, surface-level "looks good!", one-time logins

### Your Impact

Your feedback will:
- Fix bugs before public launch
- Determine which features we prioritize
- Shape the Android and iOS apps
- Influence pricing and subscription tiers
- Guide marketing and positioning

## 6. Feedback Guidelines

### Four Types of Feedback

We need four kinds of feedback from you:

#### 1. üêõ Bugs (Things that don't work)

**Examples:**
- "OCR upload button doesn't work on Safari"
- "Calendar doesn't save scheduled workouts"
- "AI workout generator returns empty workout"
- "Can't delete exercise from workout"

**What to include in bug reports:**
- **What you did**: Step-by-step actions
- **What you expected**: What should have happened
- **What actually happened**: The actual result
- **Browser/device**: Chrome, Safari, Firefox? Desktop or mobile?
- **Screenshot/video**: If possible

**Template:**
```
BUG: [Short title]

Steps to reproduce:
1. Go to...
2. Click on...
3. Enter...
4. Observe...

Expected result: [What should happen]
Actual result: [What actually happened]
Browser: [Chrome 130 on Windows 11]
Screenshot: [Attach if possible]

Priority: [Critical / High / Medium / Low]
```

---

#### 2. üí° Feature Requests (Things you wish existed)

**Examples:**
- "I wish I could export workouts to PDF"
- "Would love to see a workout plan/program builder"
- "Need a way to track cardio workouts"
- "Want to share workouts with friends"

**What to include:**
- **The problem**: What current limitation frustrates you?
- **The solution**: What feature would solve it?
- **Why it matters**: How often would you use this?
- **Alternative**: How do you solve this now (if at all)?

**Template:**
```
FEATURE REQUEST: [Short title]

Problem: [What frustrates you now?]
Proposed solution: [Your idea]
Use case: [When/how often would you use this?]
Current workaround: [How do you handle this now?]

Priority: [Must-have / Nice-to-have / Low priority]
```

---

#### 3. üé® UX Feedback (Things that are confusing or could be better)

**Examples:**
- "Not sure what 'Enhance with AI' button does until I click it"
- "OCR tab is hidden - almost didn't find it"
- "Too many clicks to schedule a workout"
- "Stats page is overwhelming - too much info at once"

**What to include:**
- **Where**: Which page/feature
- **What's confusing**: Specific element or flow
- **Why it's confusing**: What's unclear or frustrating
- **Suggestion**: How would you improve it?

**Template:**
```
UX FEEDBACK: [Page/feature name]

Issue: [What's confusing or frustrating?]
Why: [What makes it unclear?]
Suggestion: [How would you improve it?]
Impact: [How much does this affect your experience? 1-10]
```

---

#### 4. üí¨ General Feedback (Overall impressions)

**Examples:**
- "I love the dark theme - perfect for late-night gym sessions"
- "AI workouts are surprisingly good quality"
- "Would use this over Strong if mobile app existed"
- "OCR is hit-or-miss on handwritten workouts"

**What to include:**
- **What you like**: Positive highlights
- **What you don't like**: Things that bother you
- **Would you pay for this?**: Honest answer + why
- **How does it compare?**: To Strong, Hevy, FitBod, etc.

**Template:**
```
GENERAL FEEDBACK

Likes:
- [Feature/aspect you enjoy]
- [What's working well]

Dislikes:
- [What bothers you]
- [What's not working]

Would I pay?: [Yes/No/Maybe]

Compared to [Strong/Hevy/etc]: [Your comparison]

```

### Priority Levels

Help us prioritize by rating issues:

**Critical (P0):**
- App is unusable or broken
- Data loss or corruption

## 11. Glossary

**1RM (One-Rep Max):** The maximum weight you can lift for one repetition of an exercise.

**AMRAP:** "As Many Rounds (or Reps) As Possible" - a workout format where you complete as many rounds as you can in a set time.

**AWS Bedrock:** Amazon's AI service that powers our AI features (workout generation, enhancement).

**DynamoDB:** Amazon's NoSQL database where all user data is stored securely.

**EMOM:** "Every Minute On the Minute" - a HIIT format where you perform an exercise at the start of every minute.

**OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** Technology that reads text from images (like workout screenshots).

**PR (Personal Record):** Your best performance for a specific exercise (heaviest weight, most reps, etc.).

**Superset:** Performing two exercises back-to-back without rest.

**Tabata:** A HIIT protocol: 20 seconds work, 10 seconds rest, 8 rounds (4 minutes total).

**WOD (Workout of the Day):** A daily workout suggestion generated by AI based on your profile.
